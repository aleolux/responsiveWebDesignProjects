My Tree House

You dreamed it. We built it.

It is difficult to find someone who has never dreamed of building or having a tree house to call their own. The idea of a refuge, a space fully integrated with nature and with a privileged view, pleases almost all ages.

Architecture and design.

Treehouse architecture and design surveys structures constructed around, next to or among the trunk or branches of a tree, looking at contemporary projects and the diverse materials and unconventional forms employed in their realization.
